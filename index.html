My name is Nídia Sequeira Trovão and I am currently a Postdoctoral fellow of Professor Adolfo García-Sastre's laboratory at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, USA. Here, I have been part of the Centers for Excellence in Influenza Research, a global research network funded by the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases at the National Health Institutes, USA. I have also been working closely with Dr. Martha Nelson at the Fogarty International Center, studying the evolution of influenza in animal populations. For further information or if you have some "juicy" heterochronous sequence data and you’re up for a collaboration, please don’t hesitate to contact me!
