My name is Nídia Sequeira Trovão and I am currently a Postdoctoral fellow of Professor Adolfo García-Sastre's laboratory at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, USA. Here, I have been part of the Centers for Excellence in Influenza Research, a global research network funded by the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious at the National Health Institutes, USA. I have also been working closely with Dr. Martha Nelson at the Fogarty International Center, studying the evolution of influenza in animal populations.
